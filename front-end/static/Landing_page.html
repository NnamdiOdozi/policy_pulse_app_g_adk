Condition meets the legal definition of a Disability

The Manager's Playbook

DO ✔

  • Build trust with regular 1-1s.
  • Ask open, gentle questions.
  • Listen with empathy.
  • Signpost to official support.
  • Maintain confidentiality.

DON'T ✖

  • Make assumptions.
  • Ask intrusive medical questions.
  • Breach confidentiality.
  • Try to diagnose.
  • Jump to performance management.
